About

Our challenge was to create a solution where people don't loose track of their organization flow and stay sane. During times like this it is crucial to make sure that there is still room left to complete your tasks. In addition to that our goal is to design a technical solution so that people can be apart of the digital transformation.

The main goal

The goal was to create a idea and website that presented a solution for those stuck working at home. In addition we also had other ideas such as a application, google extension and website that not only has self care tips but creative solutions to everyday problems.

Aditional features

Even though the digital life has taken over most of our routine that doesn't mean we are like that! Our additional features include some products you can keep! For example a smart water bottle, motivational plush, and subscription box goodie that can be delivered faster than speed of light.
